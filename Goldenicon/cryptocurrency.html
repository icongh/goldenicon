Cryptocurrency

What is Cryptocurrency

Cryptocurrency is a digital payment system that doesn't rely on any bank to verify transactions. It is a peer-to-peer system that can make one to send or transfer funds and receive payments.
Cryptocurrency, sometimes called crypto is any form of currency that exist digitally or virtually and uses cryptography to secujre transactions.

Cryptocurrency receives it's name because it uses encryption to verify transactions
The first cryptocurrency was Bitcoin which was introduce and founded in the year 2009 and retains it popularity.

How Does The Cryptocurrency Works

Cryptocurrency runs on a distributed public ledger called Blockchain, a record of all transactions updated and held by currency holders.

Units of cryptocurrency are created through a process called mining, which involves using the computer power to solve complex mathematical problems that generate coins.
Individuals can also buy the coins from the brokers, store and spend them using cryptographic wallets.
